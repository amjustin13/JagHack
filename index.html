Frequently Asked Questions

We don't know the meaning of life but we hope to answer everything else!

Who can attend?

This is our first hackathon and we want all IUPUI students to part of it! This event is for experienced hackers and beginners alike. To give equal opportunity to all hackers, admission will be based on a lottery of applicants.

What will I eat?

Thanks to our amazing sponsors we will be providing food, snacks and drinks (non-alcoholic... sorry) to all hackers during the 24 hour competition. While you focus on crating the most amazing hacks the world has ever seen we will focus on making sure you are fuelled up and ready to go!

What should I bring?

Bring your IUPUI issued JagTag, a laptop and your charger. That's all that you really need! Some other things we suggest that you bring are: a sleeping bag, a fresh set of clothes and a tooth brush. Although we would love to see your juiced up desktop we would preffer that you leave it at home(we have limited outlets and space).

How much will it cost me?

JAGHACK is absolutely free to all hackers that are accepted to the competition! Register online, get selected, come over, hack, win some prizes maybe. Does not get much better than that!

How do teams work?

Teams work two ways: register Han style, or register with your squad. Teams will have a maximum of 4 participants. If one of the team members is selected in the lottery, all participants become admited to the event. You create a team by specifying the same team name during registration phase. If you preffer to register Solo you will have your chance to meet your Chewy during the first hours of the event.

What should I make?

Anything that starts with: "Wouldn't it be cool if..." That awesome project that you have had in the backburner for a while... Creative and innovative hacks are the life of the hackathon culture, open your mind and explore new things! You can come with an idea in mind, but you should not start building or coding anything before the event starts. All projects at JAGHACK should be started at the event.
